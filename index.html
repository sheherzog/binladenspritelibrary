Welcome to The Bin Laden Sprite Library! 

Welcome to the library, an archive of flash games made after 9/11. You will find a couple things here:
You will see simulated gore, naked expressions of Islamophobia and racism, support of war crimes and imperialism; and the pixelated dehumanization and dancing avatars of America's monsters to mask our own fears. 

Our illusion of being a principled and protected country broke, and netizens opened a new window of Adobe Flash, and built a new story, frame by frame, where they could kill and maim who they assumed was solely responsible.    

We tell this story, fragmented, here in this website. What is missing from these games is the estimated 4.5 million people who have died in post-9/11 war zones as a result of war and its impacts that ripple like a wave. (see Sawell, Stephanie, How Death Outlives War on further links page). 
When you face Him in these games, 
when you blow Him up, 
when you gut Him like a fish, 
when you tie Him to a rocket and watch him soar across the MS paint sky, 

think of those who lie in the dirt and the ash, because we dreamed of ridding Him from our world, by any means necessary.

Who deserves to be remembered? Who earns the right to memory?
